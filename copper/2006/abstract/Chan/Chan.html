Preconditioning analysis for weighted Toeplitz regularized least squares problems

Chan Lung Chak
Dept. of Mathematics, The University of Hong Kong
Pokfulam Road, Hong Kong
lungchak@graduate.hku.hk
We consider the iterative solutions of weighted Toeplitz regularized least squares (WTRLS) problems. Our approach is based on an augmented system formulation. We focus our attention on two types of preconditioners: constraint preconditioner and the Hermitian and skew-Hermitian splitting preconditioner. Numerical results are presented to illustrate and analyze the performance and limitations of the preconditioners with explanations based on some existing theoretical results. Although the analysis is for WTRLS problems, it applies also to problems with similar preconditioning techniques in a more general context.
