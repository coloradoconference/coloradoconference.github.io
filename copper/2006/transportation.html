TRANSPORTATION

Copper Mountain is located 75 miles west of Denver via Interstate 70, at exit 195. Please make your airline reservation destination Denver International Airport. All major car rental agencies have counters at the airport.

We will be arranging for group discounted rates with Resort Express shuttle service. You must identify yourself as attending the ``CU Math Conference'' to receive whatever group rates are arranged.

Reservations should be made by calling: 1-800-334-7433 or direct 970-468-7600, or fax: 970-468-0364. Resort Express is located on level 5, the baggage claim level, in the center of the main terminal.

In order to receive a full refund for a cancellation, cancellations must be received more than 24 hours in advance of scheduled trip.
