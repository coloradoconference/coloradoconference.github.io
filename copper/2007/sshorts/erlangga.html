In this paper an iterative method to solve the Helmholtz equation

Since the Helmholtz equation with high wavenumber arises problems in both smoothing and coarse grid correction, direct application of a standard multigrid method to (1) usually leads to a diverging method. We find that adding an imaginary shift to the Helmholtz equation may result in a good operator that multigrid can handle it efficiently, and that at the same time can also be used as an effective preconditioner for (1).
