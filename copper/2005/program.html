2005 PROGRAM (Student Paper Competition in Italics)


This program is preliminary and is subject to possible modification.
Times given here are only estimates and are likely to change.

Program Chairs will adhere strictly to this schedule.
Wednesday night's cash bar & banquet is in the Bighorn Room in the Copper Conference Center.
All other activities will be held in the Copper Conference Center.
