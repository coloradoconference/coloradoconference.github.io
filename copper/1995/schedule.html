CCMM95 SCHEDULE

Below is a list of sessions, session times, and talks. Each
speaker should plan for 20 minutes of lecture plus 10 minutes
of questions and transition.  The session chair will militantly
enforce the schedule.  Exact speaker times within each session
will be set and posted the night before to account for no-shows.

Morning sessions include one-half hour coffee breaks at 10:00 a.m.
