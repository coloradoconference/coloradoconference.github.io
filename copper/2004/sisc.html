Special issue of SIAM J. Sci. Comp.

The 8th annual Copper Mountain Conference on Iterative Methods is scheduled from March 28 - April 2, 2004. As in previous even-numbered years, a Special Issue of SIAM J. on Scientific Computing (SISC) is planned. This single issue will be dedicated to recent progress in iterative methods. Submission information is available at SISC's Copper Mountain Special Issue page.. The deadline for submission is May 15, 2004. Attendees and participants of the conference are invited to submit papers as well as the general community.
