CONFERENCE REGISTRATION

Either register online, or print and complete the registration form to submit by fax or postal mail. In either case, your payment must be received on or before 22 February to receive the discounted rates.

Sending your name by using the on-line registration will get you on the registration list, but your fee depends on whether or not you make the early registration deadline of February 22. If you are paying by check, please mail it with your form. You will receive a discounted rate if your payment is postmarked before February 22. The only types of payments accepted will be VISA, MASTER CARD, or CHECK (money order). We DO NOT accept American Express or Discover.

Registration fees include a registration packet, the Handbook of Abstracts, a reception on Sunday evening, continental breakfast each day, coffee breaks each morning and afternoon, and a banquet on Wednesday evening.

Register in one of these three ways:

  • Register online

  • Print and complete a registration form and mail (with payment) to
    COPPER MT. CONFERENCE
    FRONT RANGE SCIENTIFIC COMPUTATIONS
    8865 E. CALLE BUENA VISTA
    SCOTTSDALE, AZ 85255

  • Print and complete a registration form, then scan and email as a PDF document to
    copper@colorado.edu
    (be sure to submit payment, separately)
