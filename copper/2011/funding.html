CMCMM 2011

Travel Support for Students, Postdocs, Women, Minorities, and Disabled.

Support for travel, lodging, and registration is available for students and postdocs. Additional funds are available for women, minority, and disabled participants. Applications for support should be emailed to the conference coordinator, Annette Anthony (annette@gunnison.com), and must include:
  1. A title and one-paragraph abstract for a proposed talk.
  2. A brief letter of support from an advisor (students and postdocs only).
  3. An estimate of your travel expenses.
  4. A statement indicating your status (students and postdocs indicate year in program or indicate if female, minority, or disabled).
Please email this information to Annette by February 13, 2011. Requests submitted past this deadline may be considered, but only if funding is still available.
Please note that the travel support is available only for participants from U.S. institutions.
