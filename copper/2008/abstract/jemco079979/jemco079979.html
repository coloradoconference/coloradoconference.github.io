The vector extrapolation method is applied to problems in Computational Fluid Dynamics and it is shown that it leads to a significant acceleration of the convergence rates. Moreover, the method can be applied to the acceleration of any fixed-point algorithm with the minimal or no changes to the original algorithm. Results are demonstrated for the cases of the compressible and incompressible flows as well in conjunction with the explicit and implicit algorithms.
