Iterative Solver for the Finite Difference Time Domain Method with Application of Thin Film Solar Cell Simulation

Martensstrasse 3
University Erlangen-Nuremberg
Department of Computer Science
D-91058 Erlangen
pflaum@informatik.uni-eralngen.de

To optimize the optical properties of thin film solar cells it is necessary to simulate the optical properties of the nanostructure of these solar cells. The finite difference time domain method for Maxwell's equations is a suitable method for such simulations. To reduce the computational amount fast iterative solvers are needed.
