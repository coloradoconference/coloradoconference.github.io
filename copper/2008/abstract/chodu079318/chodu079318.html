Preconditioning for linear elasticity problem based on generalized finite element method

Department of Mathematics
The Pennsylvania State University
University Park
PA 16802
cho@math.psu.edu
James Brannick
Jinchao Xu
Ludmil Zikatanov

We propose and analyze a uniform preconditioner for the linear elasticity problem for a homogeneous isotropic compressible elastic materials. The construction of the preconditioner is based on a subspace correction method employed in solving the generalized finite element method (GFEM) discretization of a scalar PDE on unstructured simplicial meshes.
