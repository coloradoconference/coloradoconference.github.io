TRANSPORTATION

Copper Mountain is located 75 miles west of Denver via Interstate 70, at exit 195. Please make your airline reservation destination Denver International Airport. All major car rental agencies have counters at the airport.

We have arranged for shuttle service from the Denver International Airport at group discounted rates with Colorado Mountain Express. Be sure to mention that you are traveling with the "CU Math Group" to receive the 10% discount on your fare. Colorado Mountain Express can be reached by phone at

  • (800) 525-6363
  • (970) 926-9800
    For additional information, please visit www.ridecme.com

    In order to receive a full refund for a cancellation, cancellations must be received more than 24 hours in advance of scheduled trip.
