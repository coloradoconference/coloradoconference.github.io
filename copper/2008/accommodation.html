ACCOMMODATION AT COPPER MOUNTAIN

A lodge room has one or two queen beds, and a two-bedroom condominium has one or two queen beds in each bedroom, two baths, a kitchen, a dining area, and fireplace. Please be sure to verify the number of beds with your reservationist.

Per night rates are as follows:

	Hotel Room              $122/night
        Telemark Studio         $130/night
	One Bedroom Condo       $144/night
	Two Bedroom Condo       $249/night
Make your room reservation directly with Copper Mountain before or on March 5, 2008. Availability of rooms cannot be guaranteed after March 5--Copper Mountain reserves the right to release part of our room block if 50% of our block is not reserved at this time. After March 5, all remaining unreserved rooms will be released from our group. In previous years, we have indeed run out of rooms.

Please reserve your lodging as soon as possible!

State and local taxes (13.275%) will be added to the cost of the room. If available, group rates will be given 2 days before and after the conference. Identify yourself with the 2008 CU Math Conference Group code number 3FW3RR to get group rates. At time of reservation, a credit card number can hold the room, or you will be asked to send in 1 night's deposit within 14 days.
